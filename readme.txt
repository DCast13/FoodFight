Description:
Welcome to **Food Fight**, a hilarious and action-packed fighting game where you take control of your favorite food characters to battle it out in randomized arenas. Defeat your opponent using strategic movements and a variety of attacks to claim victory in this culinary combat!

How to Play:

**Player 1 Controls**:
- Movement: `WASD`
- Light Attack: `R`
- Medium Attack: `T`
- Heavy Attack: `Y`

**Player 2 Controls**:
- Movement: Arrow Keys
- Light Attack: `M`
- Medium Attack: `N`
- Heavy Attack: `B`

**If using gaming controllers**
- Movement: D-Pad
- Attacks: Top 3 face buttons

Additional Controls:
- Select (Main Menu & Character Select): `R`

**Objective**:
Outmaneuver your opponent and deplete their health bar to win the match. Randomized arenas provide unique challenges in every fight.

**Stages/Arenas**:
The battlefield is randomly selected each round from a variety of stages, such as: 
- Kitchen Chaos
- Picnic Mayhem
- Salad Bar Brawl

**Cheat Codes**:
- **Alt + P**: Restart the fight.
- **Alt + O**: Go to the main menu.
- **Alt + L**: Go to the character select screen.
- **Alt + I**: Instantly defeat Player 2.
- **Alt + U**: Instantly defeat Player 1.

Enjoy the food fight and prove that your dish reigns supreme!
